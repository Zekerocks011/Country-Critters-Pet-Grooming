Our Staff

Erin Keyser

Owner/Groomer

My name is Erin Keyser, and I am the owner/groomer here at Country Critters Pet Grooming. I graduated from the Michigan School of Canine Cosmetology back in March of 2003. I have been in the field for over 17 years. Over the course of my career I was a grooming instructor where I had attended school. I worked in a veterinary clinic grooming animals with special needs and animals that needed sedation for grooming. I attended Cedar Valley College majoring in Veterinary Technology . I am Pet CPR and First Aid Certified. I am a registered member of the NDGAA and IJA grooming associations. I am continuing to further my education by starting my Master Grooming certification process which I hope to have completed by end of 2020. I frequent grooming expos around the US and I also am a Competitive Groomer. Country Critters Pet Grooming has over 40 years of grooming experience between our 4 groomers. I decided to open up my own grooming shop here in Saint Johns, Michigan where I live with my husband and two sons. I have a Brittany named Emma, a Bichon named Eivin, and a black cat named Jack-O. I look forward to sharing my knowledge and experience with you and your furry friends!
